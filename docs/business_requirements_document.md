# Zevian: Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## 1. Business Objective
To streamline performance management by leveraging AI to provide consistent, objective, and real-time feedback, ultimately increasing organizational productivity and employee alignment.

## 2. Problem Statement
Traditional performance reviews are often:
- **Subjective**: Prone to manager bias.
- **Delayed**: Occurring quarterly or annually, making feedback less relevant.
- **Time-Consuming**: Requiring significant manual effort from managers to read and grade reports.

## 3. Business Requirements

### 3.1 Organizational Alignment
- The system must allow organizations to define their own custom metrics and goal weights to align with their unique business objectives.
- Support for multi-tenancy to ensure data isolation between different clients.

### 3.2 Operational Efficiency
- Automate the initial grading and summarization of employee reports.
- Provide a centralized dashboard for managers to identify "at-risk" projects without reading every report manually.

### 3.3 Talent Retention & Growth
- Provide employees with immediate, actionable feedback after report submission.
- Track performance trends to identify high-potentials and those needing support.

### 3.4 Scalability
- The architecture must support rapid growth in the number of users and organizations without performance degradation.
- AI integration must be cost-effective and scalable.

## 4. Compliance & Security
- **Data Privacy**: Strict isolation of organization data.
- **Access Control**: Role-based access control (RBAC) to protect sensitive performance data.
- **Auditability**: Track changes to scores and feedback.

## 5. Strategic Roadmap
- **Phase 1**: Core reporting and AI evaluation (Current).
- **Phase 2**: Advanced analytics and integration with third-party tools (Slack, Jira).
- **Phase 3**: AI-driven career pathing and skill gap analysis.
